Mindfulness Chime



This Mindfulness Chime helps you with a gentle nudge to bring your mind back to the present and be more aware of what you are doing. Especially when you can be easily distracted online.

Setup a recurring bell it will alert you with a chime or sound and optionally a notification. You can setup different sounds for different intervals and realize time flowing in your own way.

Being mindful is hard. This Mindfulness Bell / Chime is a small tool to help you reap its benefits. Hope you find this useful!

I wrote the first version to generate a recurring sound to keep the connection alive to my bluetooth speaker.
As a side effect, realized the chime sounds improved my focus and helped me stay on track.
Now I use this a lot, so decided to share!


Why not an extension ? Pinning this as tab makes it easy to keep it running all the time and makes it easy to mute / unmute the tab.
