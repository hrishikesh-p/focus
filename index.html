Mindfulness Chime



Being mindful is hard, but practising mindfulness has great results. This chime generator helps those who need a little nudge to bring their mind back to the present and be more aware of what they are doing. Especially when you can get distracted online very easily

The Mindfullness Chime allows you to experience time in a very different way. Each time the chime goes off , you can use that signal to bring your focus back to what you were doing. As you start experience time in terms of chimes, you will realize the passing of time feel slower or faster based on what you are doing. Realize the time interval expand or contract based on your interest.

You can use it for meditation as meditation bell. Or for mindfulness while you work on something else.
